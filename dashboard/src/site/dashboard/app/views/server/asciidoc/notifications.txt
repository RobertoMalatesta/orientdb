Notifications
-------------

Notifications panel is similar to Log panel.

In this case you can search all notifications in Workbench databases just combining the search according to the date
and time and the log type.


.Notifications panel
image::{icondir}notifications.png[width="80%"]


NOTE: To free up disk space, you can delete all the logs from the database by clicking the button
"Purge notifications now".
